## [Project Description]

RamenDesk Software focus on the things that could help university students and lecturers:
1. Students can view learning videos uploaded by lecturers and students can like, dislike or comment under the videos.
2. Lecturers can upload teaching videos.
3. Students can view and share their notes with peers.
4. Students can practice and download the past year paper as well as the answer schema provided.
5. Lecturers can upload past year papers and answer schemas.
6. Students can practice full-format practice module as well as download them in pdf format.
7. Lecturers can manage their timeslots by inserting their available timeslot or removing the unavailable timeslot.
8. Students can view and book consultation time with lecturers.
9. Students can organize their schedule such as examination dates, assignment deadlines, and personal academic tasks.

## [Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) Overview]

1. Planning:
In this planning phase, our team identified the academic challenges faced by university students and lecturers and gathered all the requirements for RamenDesk software from stakeholders. Moreover, we also created a trello board to manage tasks and track sprint progress.

2.Analysis
In this analysis stage, we analysed and documented the functional and non-functional requirements. The requirements were converted into use case and class diagram to model RamenDesk software structure and interactions.

3. Design:
For design part, wireframes of RamenDesk were created to visualize the system interface and navigation structure. This stage helped the team to have a blue print for the design of RamenDesk prototype which acts as a guide.

4.Prototype development
In this stage, an interactive prototype was developed by using Figma. It stimulates the main features of RamenDesk and allows users to navigate through RamenDesk to demonstrate how the software would work in real scenario.

5. Testing:
For testing stage, user acceptance testing (UAT) were carried out by sharing the prototype link to 13 intended users. The feedback was collected by using Google Form questionnaire. The results were used to evaluate RamenDesk's usability and functionality.

6. Final:
After the protoype was finalised, our team proceeded with our project documentation and presentation.
